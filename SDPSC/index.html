About Us

SDPS chhapa was established as a coeducational school in village Chhapa near holy place Gurudwara Kala Mala sahib in the year 1990. By the relentless of great visionary, the Late Sh. Jaswinder singh khangura founder principal, the school reached dazzling heights and became a name to reckon with in the fields of education in all over Punjab. the school admits children belonging to all the strata of the society and inculcates in them a spirit of secularism with respect for all religions.


Our Mission

To achieve excellence in learning both within the classroom and beyond. To empower teachers to guide children with means to construct knowledge. To bring back an element of joy to school surroundings which would nudge the child to grow into full bloom. To encourage children to think independently, to be passionate about what they wish to do, to value excellence. With knowledge, confidence, compassion and cheer, children must proceed to make the world a better place than they found it.
